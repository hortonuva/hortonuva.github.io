Stories told by Martile Horton McBride and Curtis McBride

These were recorded May 2000 by Tom Horton (son of John Lee Horton, Martile's brother). They were made at the McBrides house in Columbus, MT. Most of the stories concern Martile's younger days growing up in Winn Parish, LA. Also included is the story of their courtship and wedding.

Contact Tom Horton at tomhortonva(at)gmail.com for more information.
